Frequently Asked Questions

Answers about Early Access, pricing, availability and how Lumea works.

What is Lumea AI?
Lumea AI learns from your favorite portraits and gives real-time camera guidance so every selfie and portrait matches your personal style.
How does it learn my style?
Upload a few of your best portraits. Lumea analyzes lighting, angles and overall aesthetic preferences to personalize the guidance you see when the camera opens.
Does Lumea edit photos after I shoot?
Lumea focuses on live, in-camera guidance—helping you frame, light and align shots so the photo looks great before you press the shutter.
Is there an Early Access program?
Yes. Early Access for iOS is currently open and free until November 24. Use the Google Form on the homepage to join.
What will pricing be after Early Access?
Planned pricing: $7.99/month or $71.88/year (best value). Subscriptions launch soon.
Which platforms are supported?
Early Access targets iOS. We’ll share updates on other platforms on the site.
How do I join Early Access?
Go to the homepage and fill out the embedded Google Form in the Join Early Access section. We’ll get back to you as soon as possible.
How are my portraits handled?
Portraits are used to personalize your guidance experience. We’ll publish full privacy and data handling details before the subscription launch.
